EXPLORE MoMa
Published: Tuesday, March 6th, 2018. By Edda Sonja Gudmundsdottir
MoMA is a place that fuels creativity, ignites minds, and provides inspiration. Its extraordinary exhibitions and collection of modern and contemporary art are dedicated to helping you understand and enjoy the art of our time. Caption: The Museum of Modern Art, designed by Yoshio Taniguchi. Entrance at 53rd Street. In 1937, the Museum moved its location to a set of offices and basement galleries in the Time and Life Building in Rockefeller Center.
